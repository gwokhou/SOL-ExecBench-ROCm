# Requirements Summary

This root summary preserves cross-milestone validation guardrails while detailed
requirements remain archived under `.planning/milestones/`.

## Validation Boundaries

- CDNA3-family, CDNA4, or native-host ROCm validation expansion remains outside
  the current completed prerelease scope unless a dedicated milestone reopens
  it.
- MI300X full-suite validation on CDNA3 (`gfx942`) remains deferred until a
  complete real-hardware evidence chain exists.
- MI300X is the CDNA3 hardware target rather than a separate architecture target.
- Requires real hardware evidence before any CDNA3-family hardware-validation
  claim can be upgraded from schema/build/docs support to hardware validation.
