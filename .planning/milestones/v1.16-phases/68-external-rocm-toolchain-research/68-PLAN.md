# Phase 68 Plan: External ROCm Toolchain Research

## Scope

1. Capture primary-source research in `.planning/research/ROCM_TOOLCHAIN_ROUTING.md`.
2. Identify evidence levels: runtime, profiling, static/future, derived score.
3. Identify lifecycle states for active, migrated, deprecated, planned, rejected,
   and candidate tools.
4. Preserve references for downstream registry and documentation.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Research records source URLs and what each source contributes.
- Static Kernel Evidence is explicitly deferred to v1.17.
- Tool capability and lifecycle findings inform the implementation.
