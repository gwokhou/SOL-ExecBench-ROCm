# 190-01 Plan: Profiler artifact discovery, classification, and citations

## Objective

Fix `rocprofv3` profile artifact registration so requested successful profiler
runs discover concrete artifacts across recursive output layouts, classify
common profiler formats, preserve existing success semantics, and expose
checksummed citations to profile/profile-summary sidecars.

## Source Items Covered

- PROF-01: discover successful `rocprofv3` profile artifacts across supported
  formats and version-specific layouts.
- PROF-02: record concrete profiler artifact citations with kind, compact path,
  size, checksum, and source status.
- PROF-03: distinguish failure, unavailable profiler, no-artifact, partial
  coverage, and successful registration states with stable reason codes.
- D-01: recursive scan under `output_directory`.
- D-02: filter recursive candidates to requested prefix, recognized profiler
  names, or known `rocprofv3` structures.
- D-03: classify `rocpd`, CSV, JSON, Perfetto/PFTrace, and OTF2.
- D-04: register matching unknown profiler files as `other` without parsing
  every format.
- D-05: keep return-code-zero plus at least one artifact as `success`.
- D-06: express incomplete artifact sets through coverage, warnings, and reason
  codes rather than alternate success states.
- D-07: expose stable reason codes for failure, unavailable, no-artifact, and
  partial-coverage cases.
- D-08: compute SHA256 for registered profiler artifacts by default.
- D-09: document and account for large profiler database hash cost risk.
