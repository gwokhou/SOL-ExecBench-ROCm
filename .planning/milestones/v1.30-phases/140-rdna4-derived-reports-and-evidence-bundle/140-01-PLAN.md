# Phase 140 Plan

## Goal

Generate RDNA4 score, bound, consistency, stability, claim-upgrade, trust, and
bundle-manifest artifacts from the completed RDNA4 evidence set.

## Tasks

1. Generate derived score and bound artifacts.
   - Run `scripts/run_dataset.py --phase derived` against the Phase 138 output.
   - Emit AMD-native score report, AMD SOL bound sidecars, and SOLAR
     derivation sidecars under `out/rdna4-derived-reports`.
   - Preserve Phase 139 timing refs where available and non-authoritative
     timing blockers where not available.

2. Generate gap and sanity reports.
   - Run parity-gap report from manifest, inventory, readiness, ready subset,
     closure, and AMD score.
   - Run AMD bound sanity report against closure, traces, AMD score, AMD SOL,
     and SOLAR sidecars.

3. Generate consistency, claim-upgrade, and trust reports.
   - Feed execution closure, AMD score, AMD bound sanity, SOL/SOLAR sidecars,
     and Phase 139 stability report into the existing report scripts.
   - Ensure claim-upgrade remains blocked where timing, score, or paper parity
     prerequisites are not met.

4. Generate evidence bundle manifest.
   - Record stable refs and checksums for Phase 138/139/140 artifacts.
   - Mark local-only dataset/generated evidence boundaries.
   - Keep score, timing, paper parity, and leaderboard authority flags explicit.

5. Close Phase 140.
   - Write `140-SUMMARY.md` and `140-VERIFICATION.md`.
   - Update milestone requirements and state.

## Verification

- Derived reports exist and reference the Phase 138 closure.
- Consistency, claim-upgrade, trust, and parity reports exist.
- Evidence bundle manifest includes checksums for closure, summary, timing,
  stability, score, bound, and trust artifacts.
- Reports preserve the Phase 139 timing blockers and do not overclaim
  benchmark-grade timing or leaderboard authority.
