# Milestone Audit: v1.10 Paper-Aligned SOLAR Automatic Derivation

**Audit date:** 2026-05-23  
**Milestone:** v1.10 Paper-Aligned SOLAR Automatic Derivation  
**Scope:** Phases 47-52, v1.10 requirements, cross-phase wiring, public contracts,
claim boundaries, and explicit deferrals.

## Overall Verdict

**PASS.**

The v1.10 milestone satisfies its scoped goal: the ROCm port now has a
paper-aligned automatic SOLAR derivation path that derives internal sidecar
evidence from canonical benchmark inputs, promotes high-confidence families into
formula-backed evidence, conservatively degrades MoE and SSM/Mamba structures,
exposes machine-verifiable sidecar coverage/status, guards AMD-native scores
against unscored evidence, and integrates the flow into dataset-runner reports
without mutating canonical benchmark artifacts.

No cross-phase blocker was found. Every expected v1.10 connection resolves to
**WIRED** end-to-end.

## Public Contract Preservation

**Preserved.**

- Canonical `Definition`, `Workload`, and `Trace` schemas are guarded by exact
  key tests.
- Canonical trace JSONL excludes derived report and SOLAR sidecar key spaces,
  including `solar_derivation`, `coverage_summary`, `aggregate_status`, and
  `derived_evidence_refs`.
- Primary `sol-execbench` CLI help does not expose v1.10 dataset-runner-only
  options such as `--solar-derivation`.
- Public AMD-native score `evidence_refs` key space remains stable; new
  formula/coverage/score-eligibility references live in separate
  `derived_evidence_refs`.
- No new framework dependency such as ONNX, MLIR, Dynamo, sympy, or networkx was
  introduced.

## Review/Fix Closure

All review findings in Phases 51 and 52 were fixed and re-reviewed cleanly.

- Phase 51 blocker fixed: degraded aggregate sidecars now emit
  `score_eligible=True`, matching AMD-native score behavior for degraded but
  numerically complete evidence.
- Phase 51 warning fixed: parser now rejects supplied `coverage_summary` and
  `aggregate_status` values that semantically disagree with parsed groups and
  warnings.
- Phase 52 blocker fixed: generated SOLAR and AMD SOL v2 sidecar filenames now
  use a safe deterministic stem, preventing path traversal from definition or
  workload identifiers.
- Phase 52 warning fixed: traversal-shaped identifier regression coverage was
  added.
