<objective>

without creating a second benchmark runner or mutating canonical dataset files.
</objective>

  only under `--output-dir`.
- Runner drift: all actual benchmark attempts must continue to flow through
  `run_cli()` and the primary `sol-execbench` subprocess path.
- Filter ambiguity: every ready workload excluded by category, limit, missing
  discovery, or workload cap needs an explicit filtered/not-attempted reason.
</threat_model>
