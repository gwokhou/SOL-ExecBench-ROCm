<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| local sidecar payloads -> consistency builder | Untrusted JSON dictionaries are normalized into strict diagnostic models. |
| source refs/checksums -> renderer | User-provided refs and checksums are rendered into JSON/Markdown. |
| consistency result -> researcher interpretation | Readers may mistake internal consistency for validation authority unless boundaries are explicit. |

| T-89-01-03 | Information Disclosure | source refs/rendering | mitigate | Store compact refs/checksums only; do not embed raw sidecar payloads or logs. |

</threat_model>
